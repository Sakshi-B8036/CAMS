About Us

The College Attendance Management System (CAMS) is an advanced digital platform developed to modernize and streamline the process of tracking student attendance. Designed with precision and ease-of-use in mind, CAMS brings together administrators, teachers, and students on a unified system that enhances productivity and transparency.

Through role-based dashboards, teachers can mark attendance quickly, administrators can analyze institute-level attendance trends, and students can monitor their academic presence in real time. By eliminating manual errors and paperwork, CAMS promotes a smarter, more efficient, and digitally empowered academic environment.
