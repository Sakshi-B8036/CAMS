About Us

The College Attendance Management System (CAMS) is an intelligent web-based solution designed to simplify and automate the process of tracking student attendance. It provides a unified digital platform for administrators, teachers, and students to manage records efficiently, ensuring accuracy, transparency, and real-time accessibility. Through role-based dashboards, CAMS empowers teachers to mark attendance seamlessly, allows administrators to oversee overall statistics and performance analytics, and enables students to monitor their own progress. By replacing traditional manual methods, CAMS enhances productivity, reduces paperwork, and supports a smarter, more connected academic environment.
